How I make videos?

Creative process

This is the quick go through the creative process on how your stories turns into the videos.

For the Equipment I use with a stabilizer for casual shots. Sometimes I use for tighter areas or action shots. To take an areal footage, I mastered my skills flying the .

Meeting / Video shooting

Before shooting, I usually meet with my clients to get to know their desires better. Afterward comes the video shooting part, where I try to realize clients' ideas as best as possible.

Soundtrack

I start my video editing process by finding the right soundtrack, and as a producer, I make it attractive to catch the viewer's attention through their eyes and ears. On Soundtracks, I work with FL Studio and Adobe Audition.

Video edit / Effects

Then I start cutting the video and putting the footage onto the soundtrack that the clients already approve. When editing, I love to make an effort to ensure that my editing style fits the soundtrack perfectly that was made by looking at the footage I already shot. After a rough edit in Premiere Pro, I work on the Effects and more complex transitions in the After Effects.

Sound design / Color grade

In videography and filmmaking, the sound is as important as what we see, so I take some time after finishing the final edit to make sure that every move you see in the video that I would love to highlight, I make the sound for it. Finally, I color-grade my videos in the Premiere Pro before the final export.
